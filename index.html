WHEEL SELECTION DESK

01 · Should I sell premium at all today? — regime strip

Carry = (IV30 − HV10) / IV30 — how much 30-day implied vol exceeds recent (10-day) realized vol, as a fraction of implied; positive = options are rich vs how the stock is actually moving. This strip is the median carry across the whole watchlist, one year back; the band is where it usually lives. The only chart that can answer "nobody, come back Thursday."

02 · What kind of day is it for each name? — stretch × carry quadrants

Every name as one dot. Left–right: how oversold or overbought price is vs its own normal movement. Up–down: carry (= (IV30 − HV10)/IV30). The quadrant names the trade each name invites. Click a dot to drill in.

03 · Which name? — carry vs yield, one dot per ticker at its 25Δ strike

Every name standardized to its strike nearest 0.25 delta — equal assignment risk, so differences are purely price. Top-right: well paid, and cheap to underwrite — the stock moves less than the options imply (rich carry). Bottom-right (the trap): well paid, but the stock moves more than implied (negative carry) — you'll get run over. Click a dot to drill in.
